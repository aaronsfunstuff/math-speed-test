Math Speed Challenge - Rules

Welcome to the Math Speed Challenge! Here are the rules to help you get started:

Objective

Answer as many math questions as possible within the time limit to achieve a high score. The faster you answer correctly, the higher your score!

How to Play

  • Select a difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  • Read the math question displayed on the screen.
  • Type your answer into the input field and press "Submit" or hit Enter.
  • If your answer is correct, a new question will appear, and your score will increase.
  • If your answer is incorrect or you run out of time, the game will end.

Scoring

Your score increases by one point for each correct answer. The game ends when you submit an incorrect answer or the timer runs out.

Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: Questions with numbers between 1 and 10.
  • Medium: Questions with numbers between 1 and 50.
  • Hard: Questions with numbers between 1 and 100.

Leaderboard

Your scores will be saved on the leaderboard, where you can compare your performance with other players.
